# Image Brief — cash4homefl.com

## Best way to get images

Use this order:

1. **real local photos**
2. **licensed stock photos**
3. **simple branded graphics**
4. **AI-generated placeholders only when necessary**

For a real-estate wholesaler site, local trust matters more than fancy art.

## What images should exist

### Must-have image categories

- owner / team photo
- local neighborhood street photos
- branded vehicle / yard sign / office photo
- seller handshake or closing moment
- testimonial headshots
- before/after home photos with permission
- simple icon illustrations for process steps

## Best image strategy by page type

### Homepage

Use:
- one strong hero image
- trust badges / review logos
- one local lifestyle or neighborhood image
- one or two proof images

### City pages

Use:
- 1 local street or neighborhood image
- 1 small city map graphic or landmark image
- 1 testimonial image if available

### Situation pages

Use:
- simple, relevant imagery
- no sensational or cheesy visuals
- subtle icons or line art can work well

### Reviews page

Use:
- real seller photos if available
- otherwise clean quote cards with initials and city

## What to avoid

- fake AI house photos that look too perfect
- stock images that feel generic or corporate
- money stacks and cartoon dollar signs
- dramatic crime-scene style visuals
- low-quality screenshots

## Technical image rules

- store assets locally in `/public/images`
- use `.webp` or `.avif`
- lazy-load below the fold
- add meaningful `alt` text
- keep hero images large and compressed
- generate responsive sizes

## Suggested folder structure

- `/public/images/hero/`
- `/public/images/cities/`
- `/public/images/testimonials/`
- `/public/images/icons/`
- `/public/images/process/`

## Shot list to collect

### Priority 1

- headshot of you or your team
- branded car or shirt
- one clean local house exterior
- one seller meeting / handshake photo

### Priority 2

- 3–5 West Palm Beach neighborhood shots
- 3–5 Broward neighborhood shots
- a few “before” property condition shots
- a few “after / cleaned up” shots

### Priority 3

- testimonial portraits with permission
- office / desk / call center images
- simple graphics for process steps

## If you do not have real images yet

Use:

- a clean color block hero
- a simple local map graphic
- trustworthy iconography
- one neutral stock image at most

Do not fake a local brand with random stock photos.

## AI image guidance

If you use AI images:

- keep them as backgrounds or placeholders
- do not represent them as real homes or real sellers
- do not generate fake testimonial photos
- do not use them where trust or identity matters
