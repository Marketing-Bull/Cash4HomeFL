# ICP Research — Target Zip Codes (33020–33025, Broward County / Hollywood FL)

## Composite ICP Profile

Based on census data across the 6 target zip codes, the ideal customer profile for Cash4HomeFL is:

- **Age:** 36–42 years old (median across zips)
- **Ethnicity:** Predominantly Hispanic (52% in 33024) and Black/African American (51% in 33023); White ~22–44% in mixed zips. The market is majority-minority.
- **Income:** $56K–$76K median household income — working-to-middle class
- **Home Values:** $339K–$469K — modest South Florida homes, often older ranch-style or 1970s–1990s construction
- **Homeownership:** 34%–75% depending on zip — many are long-term owners who may be equity-rich but cash-poor
- **Key Situations:** Inherited homes, storm/water damage, foreclosure risk, divorce, job relocation, tenant issues

## Image Direction

All hero and testimonial images should reflect:
- **Diverse, majority-minority South Florida homeowners** — Hispanic and Black families aged 35–55
- **Modest but well-kept South Florida homes** — 1970s–1990s ranch-style, stucco, tile roofs, palm trees
- **Relatable situations** — stress relief, paperwork signing, moving boxes, family moments
- **Warm, sunny South Florida light** — bright, tropical feel
- NOT: generic white suburban stock photos or luxury mansion imagery
